About Me

I’ve spent the past eight years as a private tutor and two years as a college educator. I earned my Master of Arts in Anthropology from San Diego State University, graduating summa cum laude with a perfect 4.0 GPA, and hold a BA in Biological Anthropology with a minor in Film & Electronic Media from California State University, Long Beach. I now offer one-on-one tutoring for elementary through high school students, breaking down complex ideas into clear, engaging lessons tailored to each learner’s style and goals.
